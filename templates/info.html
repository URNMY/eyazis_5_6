How to deal with application:

To make your system pronounce all the phrases you pass into the text field, you need come here "Speech synthesis" and select all the variable values to customize your voice into something that you want to hear where the following customization options will be available:

- text input, or copying through the buffer in a special field;

- adjusting (selecting) the speaker's voice, tempo, volume;

- playback of the generated speech signal for the entered text with button below.

To make your system hear you and do whatever you want with text information, that was passed in, you just need to come here "Speech synthesis" and after button clicking pronounce something in german language with code word in your message, but system can recognize and deal with specific available options, that are:

- command "sagen" - to pronounce the text after you;

- command "finden" - to open your message text in google query;

- command "zeig mir" - to just output your text on display.
